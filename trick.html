Explaination

The technique is simple: add the initial number of selected cards to determine the number you imagined.

Example

Consider the number 17:

it exists on the Cards 1,2,3, and the initial numbers of those cards are 3,4, and 5,

therefore by using the strategy, 3+4+5 = 12, which is equivalent to the number we anticipated.
